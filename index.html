Calling all runners (and walkers) near and far! It’s time to kick off the holiday season with the 20th Annual Austin Jingle Bell 5K!!! Join us on December 11th 2016 @ The Domain, Rain or Shine! Partake in the Holiday festivities that kick off at 8 am including a Chip Timed 5K and a Kids’ Fun Run!!! Please arrive and be ready to go at 7:45 am! Experience the family fun with Santa hats, jingle bells, and hanging out with Santa! The first 1,000 registrants are guaranteed our newly designed event athletic shirt that wicks away the sweat! The top 3 runners in each of the divisions will receive a medal based on their finish! All kids 12 and under will receive their very own participation medal! Don’t delay and register your family today!
